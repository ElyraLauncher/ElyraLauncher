# Brand Inspiration

## Scope

ElyraLauncher may study broad launcher UX patterns such as clear navigation, calm motion, readable surfaces, predictable settings, and efficient app discovery. Inspiration should remain general and should not copy proprietary product identity.

## Boundaries

- Do not copy proprietary layouts, icons, names, or visual systems.
- Do not import assets without a compatible license.
- Do not describe copied behavior as Elyra identity.
- Keep ElyraLauncher identity original and maintainable.

## Elyra Identity

Elyra identity should come from reviewed resources, typography decisions, icon direction, motion tuning, and system integration that fit ElyraOS. Brand work must preserve Launcher3 and Quickstep behavior.
